Exlainer Video

This is a simple explainer video for a hobby project, Expression Audio. I used After Effects for a number of the scenes and premier pro to build the complete video with transitions. In the end it was a fun simple project. I would like to explore the parallax effect further, and include some z-axis traversal through the shot.

Spotlight

Parallax Animations
The first two scenes are static images, separated into layers. Using After Effects I created a simple parallax effect, and added a rag-doll effect to the subject of each photo. In combination it creates a visually interesting slow motion effect.
Transitions
My goal for the transitions was to create a subtle effect, that also flowed nicely with both the background music and the voice-over.
